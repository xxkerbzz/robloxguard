# How do I set up parental controls on Roblox?

## Topic Foundation

Setting up parental controls on Roblox protects your child from unauthorized spending, inappropriate content, and online scams. The process involves configuring Roblox's built-in settings and optionally adding third-party monitoring for enhanced protection.

## How do I access the parental control settings?

Access parental controls by logging into your child's Roblox account on the website. Click the gear icon in the top right corner, select "Settings," then navigate to the "Privacy" and "Security" sections. These sections contain all available parental control options including account restrictions, spending limits, and privacy settings.

If you don't have access to your child's account, you'll need to create a parent account or have your child log in so you can configure the settings together.

## What settings should I configure first?

Start with privacy settings to control who can communicate with your child. Set messaging to "Friends" or "No one," disable friend requests from strangers, and restrict who can invite your child to games. Next, enable content filtering to block inappropriate games and content automatically.

Then configure spending limits in the Security section. Set a monthly Robux spending limit that requires your approval for purchases above the threshold. This prevents unauthorized spending even if your child has access to a payment method.

## How do I set up spending limits?

Set spending limits by going to Settings > Security > Monthly Spending Limit. Enter your desired monthly limit in Robux. Once this limit is reached, all further purchases are automatically blocked until you approve them or increase the limit.

Link a payment method that you control and enable purchase notifications. This ensures you receive immediate alerts about any spending activity, allowing you to review and approve or block transactions in real-time.

## Should I use third-party parental control services?

Third-party services like RobloxGuard provide enhanced protection beyond Roblox's built-in controls. These services monitor account activity externally, making them impossible for children to disable. They also offer real-time fraud detection, instant SMS alerts, and comprehensive activity reports.

While Roblox's built-in controls provide basic protection, third-party solutions are essential if you want proactive fraud prevention and cannot monitor the account constantly yourself.

## How long does the setup process take?

Basic Roblox parental controls can be configured in about 10 minutes. This includes setting privacy restrictions, enabling content filtering, and configuring spending limits. Adding a third-party monitoring service typically takes an additional 5 minutes for account integration and initial setup.

The entire process is straightforward and doesn't require technical expertise. Most services provide step-by-step guidance to help you complete the setup quickly.
